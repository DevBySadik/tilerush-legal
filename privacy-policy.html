Tile Rush - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Tile Rush does not collect any personal information directly. However, we use third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob - for displaying advertisements
  • Google Play Games - for leaderboards and achievements

2. How We Use Information

Information collected by third-party services is used solely to display relevant advertisements and provide game services.

3. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to show personalized ads. You can opt out in your device settings.

4. Children's Privacy

Tile Rush is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
